HOLD ON! WE NEED TO CHECK THAT...

As the climate change has risen as a public priority, politicians’ promise of addressing this issue is never off the stage. However, words without action never get things done. We are wondering , how do political efforts actually affect carbon emission activities?

To tackle it, 853142 climatechange-related quotations of politicians are extracted from 178 million quotations over 377 thousand web domains between August 2008 and April 2020.

Now Let’s take a first look at it!
